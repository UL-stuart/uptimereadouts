# Sonic Score Notes Feedback Themes

Source: `sonic-airtable.csv`, `Score Notes` column.

Scope: all rows in the file. There are 22 Airtable rows, 20 of which contain score notes.

## Main Themes

## Overall Readout

The score notes are broadly positive. Sonic players value drills most when they feel realistic, difficult, and directly connected to better incident response at Sonic. The clearest learning value is in practical operating habits: grounding, rollback judgment, escalation, timeboxing, impact analysis, communication, and PIR discipline. The main improvement opportunities are to keep narratives fresh, preserve technical fidelity, and provide better in-drill nudges when a player is stuck.
