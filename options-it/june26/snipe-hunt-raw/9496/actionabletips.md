# Actionable Tips

1. **Use failed rollbacks as elimination evidence sooner.** After one or two rollbacks fail, stop the rollback sequence and state what has been ruled out. Then pivot to first-failure logs and mechanism-based investigation.

2. **Name access constraints instead of re-pinging unavailable people.** Once Hamed/Tinus auto-reply, tell the team the chain is blocked and what path you will use next. This will reduce time lost to repeated unavailable channels.

3. **Avoid unsupported firm ETAs in stakeholder updates.** Your initial scope update was useful, but a firm 10-minute estimate without evidence undermines trust. Use confidence-aware updates: "No ETA yet; next update in 5 minutes after cert/log review."
