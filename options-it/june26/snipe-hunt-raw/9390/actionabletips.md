1. **Stop using change logs as rollback queues.** Review changes to eliminate candidates by mechanism first. Before rolling back, state which change could cause the observed payment failure and why; otherwise keep it in the background.

2. **Keep constraint handling decisive after the first unavailable signal.** You named access issues well, but re-pinged after auto-replies. Treat an OOO as a closed path, move to the next option, and tell the team what decision path you are now using.

3. **Add ruled-in/ruled-out summaries to the technical channel.** You delegated well, but the team needs your synthesis: "DNS ruled out, deploy timings do not fit, PaymentService outbound TLS is the focus." This improves M3 and K4 together.
