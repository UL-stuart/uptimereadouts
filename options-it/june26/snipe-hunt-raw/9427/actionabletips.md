# Actionable Tips

1. **State eliminations so the team stops circling back.** Once complaints predate maintenance, say: "Maintenance timing does not fit; email is out of scope unless new evidence appears." This turns evidence into narrowing and reduces repeated checks.

2. **Resolve approval deadlocks faster.** When Tinus/Hamed are unreachable and Bez cannot approve technical work, name the exhausted chain and make the ownership call sooner. A clear "I accept the consequences and authorise this" should come before the team has to prompt repeatedly.

3. **Replace generic asks with focused investigation requests.** Instead of "check all possible platforms" or "any more leads?", assign targeted evidence work: "Daniel, isolate first PaymentService TLS failure; Tanya, compare loaded cert vs bundle; Shay, track customer-facing comms."
