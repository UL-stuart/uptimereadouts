# Actionable Tips

1. **Close disproven leads out loud.** When email maintenance or recent changes are cleared, post the elimination: "Email is ruled out because it is not on the payment path; focus is now PaymentService outbound TLS." This prevents revisiting dead leads and improves team orientation.

2. **Batch requests to constrained teammates.** When Tanya or approvers are unavailable, state the constraint once and collect your asks before interrupting. This protects scarce bandwidth while keeping your delegation targeted.

3. **After a failed fix, identify the new failure mode before asking for ideas.** Replace "any ideas?" with "the restart changed the error from expiry to chain verification; what dependency does that imply?" That makes adaptation more diagnostic and less teammate-led.
