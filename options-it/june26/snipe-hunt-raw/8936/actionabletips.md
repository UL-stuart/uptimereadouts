# Actionable Tips

1. **Use mechanism checks before acting on correlations.** Before rolling back or restarting based on timing, say the causal path aloud: "I think X caused Y because Z." If you cannot name the mechanism, keep the lead open but lower its priority and ask for targeted evidence instead.

2. **Turn failed remediation into a diagnostic pause.** When a rollback or restart does not work, ask whether the error is the same or different before trying another similar action. This will help you spot hidden coupling, such as certificate bundle dependencies, faster.

3. **Make short synthesis updates for both teams.** Every few exchanges, post what is known, what has been ruled out, and what is being checked next. This will strengthen K2/K4 performance by giving business stakeholders useful substance and keeping the technical team aligned.
