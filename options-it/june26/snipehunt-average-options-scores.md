## Calculation Method

Marker category averages were calculated from the individual marker score columns:

- **Leadership:** marker columns beginning with `L`
- **Coordination:** marker columns beginning with `C`
- **Mindset:** marker columns beginning with `M`
- **Communication:** marker columns beginning with `K`

Facet averages were calculated directly from each facet column, `F1` through `F5`. All averages below are across the 15 included sessions.
