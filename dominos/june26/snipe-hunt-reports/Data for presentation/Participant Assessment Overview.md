## Executive Summary

The Domino’s cohort demonstrates measurable improvement across incident leadership, communication, coordination, and confidence. Coordination emerged as the strongest capability area, with participants reporting better stakeholder management, faster escalation, and stronger cross-functional collaboration.

Beyond incident response, several participants highlighted positive impacts on project delivery, problem-solving, and operational teamwork. These improvements suggest that the programme is influencing broader operational behaviours, not just incident management performance.

Participants such as Vallabh reported adopting a more structured approach to incident investigation, focusing on customer impact, revenue implications, and business context rather than purely technical troubleshooting. This reflects growing maturity in incident management thinking across the cohort.

Future development should focus on strengthening independent incident leadership and providing deeper technical training in root cause analysis, troubleshooting, and complex decision-making scenarios. Overall, the programme is delivering meaningful capability growth and improving operational readiness across the Domino’s technology teams.
